**WebTech Final Project** 🚀

Hi! Welcome to our Final Project. 
We are two BLIS (Bachelor of Library and Information Science) students 
who wanted to build a simple, clean, and interactive portfolio to share 
a bit about who we are.

**What's in here?**

We built this site to showcase our profiles, what we like 
to do in our free time, our goals, and how you can get in touch with us. 

**You'll find:**

Student Profiles: A look at who we are and what we're studying.  
Hobbies Table: A quick breakdown of the things we enjoy doing outside of school.  
Goals: A list of what we're working toward as we start our journey in BLIS.  
Contact: How to reach us if you have any questions.  

**The Tech Stuff** 💻

We kept things straightforward but functional by using:
HTML5 to structure everything.  
Bootstrap 5 to make sure the site looks good on your phone or your laptop.  
Custom CSS (style.css) to give it our own personal touch.
